Control Systems and Artificial Intelligence

This blog's purpose is education and knoweledge sharing. Control systems are everywhere. Human body is the greatest control system to ever exist. Our vision sensors feed the vision data to our brain and our brain (The controller tuned by nature and experience) generates the required response for every part of our body. I am very passionate about control systems and Artificial Intelligence. I hope to share and in doing so learn more about this through this blog. Topic suggestions are welcome.
